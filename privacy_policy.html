Introduction

SurveySync is Software as a Service (SaaS) based company that provides its clients tools to conduct two-way communication with their customers, employees, suppliers, and constituents. At SurveySync, protecting the privacy of our clients, survey participants, and visitors is a core business principle. Like the postal service, we understand that the information we deliver belongs to our clients and their survey participants, not us.

Our practices stated in this Privacy Policy are applicable worldwide. SurveySync is a global organization, with legal entities, business processes, management structures, and technical systems that cross borders. We may share information between SurveySync entities and transfer it between our offices around the world. Some countries may provide less legal protection for personal information than the country in which the information was obtained. Even if a country does not have stringent requirements for protecting your privacy, we still handle information in the manner described here.

This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by SurveySync on our website at the URL https://www.SurveySync.com/ (the “Site”), our SurveySync service platform owned and operated by SurveySync LLC (“SurveySync”, “we”, “us”, “our”), and any other service or product provided by SurveySync that explicitly specifies the applicability of this Privacy Policy (collectively, “Service(s)”). This Privacy Policy does not apply to data collected on third party websites, apps, or services, or the use of SurveySync’s platform and services by third parties for collection of information, even if such third parties are SurveySync’s partners and display SurveySync branding on their websites, apps, or services. Data collected by third parties is subject to their own privacy policies and practices. This Privacy Policy describes how SurveySync collects, receives, uses, stores, shares, discloses, transfers, and processes your personal information (collectively, “Process”, “Processed”, “Processing”), as well as your rights and choices in determining what we do with the information that we collect or hold about you. This Privacy Policy does not govern the Processing of non-personal or anonymized information by SurveySync.

This Privacy Policy is a part of SurveySync’s Terms of Service. If any word or phrase has been defined within the Terms of Service, but has not been defined within this Privacy Policy, that word or phrase shall have the meaning that has been assigned to it under the Terms of Service.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@SurveySync.com.

1. What Information does SurveySync collect?

SurveySync collects information about: (1) our customers (“Clients”); (2) the individuals who participate in, or are invited to participate in, any of our Clients’ surveys (“Survey Participants”); and (3) any visitor to our Site, apps, platforms, and Services, whether or not they are Clients, Survey Participants, or other visitors (collectively, “Visitors”). Collectively, the information collected about our Clients (“Client Information”), Survey Participants (“Participant Information”), and Visitors (“Visitor Information”) is referred to as “Information”.

1.1 All Visitors:

If you choose to use our referral service on SurveySync to share an article from our Service, we will ask you for your email address and your recipient’s email address. We will automatically send your recipient a one-time email in your name inviting him or her to visit the Site or Service to view the article.

We collect certain information in accordance with the Section on “Cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies” below.

If you share any personally identifiable information through comments or forms on our blog, forum, or public resources, it may be read, collected, or used by third parties to send unsolicited messages to you. You assume sole responsibility for the use of any personally identifiable information that you choose to submit in public view. To request removal of your personal information from our blogs, forum, or public resources, contact us at privacy@SurveySync.com. In some cases, we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why. We encourage all users to exercise caution when providing personal information in blogs, forums, and public posts.

You can import contacts from Gmail or other address books to invite other individuals to become SurveySync users. We do not collect the username and password for the email account from which you wish to import your contacts, and we only collect the contact email address for contacts that you select for that purpose.

1.2 Clients:

When our Clients register on the Service, we ask for contact information including company name, name of contact, title of contact, telephone number, email address, User ID, password, and billing information which may include credit card numbers. We may ask our Clients to provide additional categories of information as mentioned in the sign-up form. Clients are required to provide us with Information that is necessary for us to provide our Services.

Clients can log in to our Service using sign-in services such as Facebook Connect, Google Sign-in, or an Open ID provider. These services will authenticate your identity and provide you the option to share certain Information with us, such as your name and email address to pre-populate our sign-up form. Services like Facebook Connect give you the option to post information about your activities on this Service to your profile page to share with others within your network.

1.3 Survey Participants:

We receive Participant Information from both Clients and Survey Participants. Clients provide us with contact information such as email address, and sometimes additional details including the name of each Participant. Before receiving such Information, we contractually require Clients to warrant that they have a pre-existing relationship with the Survey Participant and/or the Survey Participant’s permission to receive electronic messages. Survey Participants provide us with the Information requested by our Clients as well as all answers to the survey questions. Clients are the sole responsible party for the content of the surveys; We do not control the theme or questions asked in any survey. In order to enhance survey response rates, we track and record: (I) if and when a Survey Participant has responded to a survey and (ii) if and when a Survey Participant has received an invitation or a reminder to participate in a survey.

We use Client Information to provide our services to our Clients, Participant Information in accordance with Client instructions, and Visitor Information to improve the features and security of our Site and Services. We use personal information strictly in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If SurveySync intends to use any personal information in a manner that deviates from this Privacy Policy, we will provide a notice of how we intend to use that Information as we collect it. The use of that specific Information will be limited to the use stated in the alternative disclosure.

2.1 All Visitors: We may use your Information:

For our legitimate interests: To protect our legal rights, to improve the performance, features and security of our Site and Services by analyzing user behavior, or for detection or prevention of fraud or misuse of our Services or properties.

For compliance with our legal obligations: For law enforcement purposes, for compliance with a legal requirement or court order.

2.2 Clients: We use Client Information to:

2.2.1. To fulfil our obligations to you

To provide our services in the normal course of our business, including to contact you regarding technical support, discuss billing issues, or to bring to your attention any aspect of our mutual business such as changes to our agreements or this privacy policy.

To provide you strictly service-related announcements when we consider it necessary to do so. For instance, we might send you an email if our service is suspended for maintenance. You may not opt-out of these necessary and non-promotional emails without terminating your account.

Upon receiving a request for help or support from a Client, SurveySync's authorized employees may, without seeking any additional permission or consent, undertake the following actions: (i) Access that Client’s account for the sole and limited purpose of providing help and support to the Client or their Survey Participants as per their request; (ii) Contact the Client's Survey Participants for investigation of issues; and (iii) Make necessary changes to resolve the issue.

2.2.2. For our legitimate interests:

To provide you with product updates or to introduce new products and services. If you do not wish to receive promotional emails from us, please follow the unsubscribe instructions included in each promotional email.

2.3 Survey Participants:

SurveySync collects personal Information on its service platform under the direction of its Clients and has no direct relationship with the individuals whose personal data it processes. If you are a customer of one of our Clients and would no longer like to be contacted by one of our Clients that use our service, please directly contact the Client that you interact with. Personal Participant Information may be used by SurveySync to:

Send survey invitations and reminders at our Client’s direction. Fulfill the purpose for which the Client has engaged SurveySync. Troubleshoot technical problems. Resolve disputes. Address complaints. Verify compliance with the Terms of Service.

2.4 Newsletters:

If you subscribe to our newsletter(s), we will use your name and email address to send the newsletter(s) to you.

2.5 Testimonials:

We may publish your comments, reviews, feedback, or testimonials regarding the Service. Such comments, reviews, feedback, or testimonials may contain personal information that has been provided by you as a part of the comment, review, feedback or testimonial. Except when we respond to a publicly available comment, review, feedback, or testimonial, we will seek your consent prior to publicly posting your name along with the comment, review, feedback, or testimonial. Please contact us at privacy@SurveySync.com if you want us to remove any comment, review, feedback, or testimonial that contains your personal information.

SurveySync does not use or disclose personal information for purposes that are materially different from those described in this policy. We will share your personal information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy policy. SurveySync will provide individuals with reasonable mechanisms to exercise their choices.

3.1 Participant Information with Client:

Our business is the provision of tools that our Clients use to conduct meaningful surveys with their employees, suppliers, customers, and constituents. Accordingly, we provide Clients with access to Participant Information obtained in response to the surveys that Clients have conducted. Clients may use Participant Information in accordance their own privacy policy and applicable law.

3.2 As Explicitly Allowed by Clients or Participants:

We may share or otherwise use Client Information as explicitly allowed by Clients and we may share Participant Information as either the Client or the Survey Participant expressly allows. For example, Clients are given the opportunity to either hide or make available to Survey Participants certain Client contact information in connection with any survey.

3.3 Agents Acting on Our Behalf:

We may share some of your Information with other companies who provide us with technical and other services. They will be contractually granted access to only that Information which is necessary for them to perform their intended tasks and they will be prohibited from using the Information for any other purpose. We do not share any personally identifiable information with third parties who are not service providers.

3.4 For Legal Reasons:

We may disclose your personal information as required by law such as to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims and when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, or to protect your safety or the safety of others. In certain situations, SurveySync may be required to disclose personal data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements. We may also share your Information to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law.

3.5 Merger and Acquisition:

If SurveySync is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, Clients will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Site or Service of any changes in ownership, Processing of personal Information, and choices available to Clients regarding any personal Information in our possession.

3.6 Marketing:

If you are a Visitor or a Client, we may share your email address with third parties for the limited purpose of showing our advertisements to you. Where required by law, we may do so only with your consent.

Deleted data may continue to exist in our backups for disaster recovery purposes in accordance with this Section 5. 5.5 We may retain any Information until any dispute is resolved, or to protect our legal rights, or if we are obligated to do so by a law, court order, or law enforcement, or for prevention and detection of fraud or misuse of our Services or properties, or for enforcement of our agreements. 5.6 For disaster recovery, Client data back-ups are taken at regular intervals as long as the Client holds an active account with SurveySync. 5.7 Once any Information has been deleted, it cannot be recovered.
